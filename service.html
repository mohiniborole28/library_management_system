Services

Book Lending & Returns

Our library offers an efficient and user-friendly book lending system. Users can borrow a wide range of academic and general books for a specified period.

Digital Catalog Access

We provide access to an online public access catalog (OPAC) that allows users to search for books, check availability, and view their borrowing history — anytime, anywhere.

Reference & Research Assistance

Our trained staff assist users in locating reference materials and using digital databases. We help with citation support, research guidance, and academic resources.

Reading & Study Zones

Quiet and comfortable reading areas are available for focused study. Dedicated sections are provided for group discussions, project work, and digital reading through e-resources.

Latest-Services

Expanded Book Collection

Our library regularly updates its inventory with the latest academic books, journals, and literary works to meet the evolving needs of students and faculty. This service ensures access to the most relevant and current materials.

E-Resource Access Portal

We now offer seamless access to digital resources including eBooks, research databases, and online journals. This allows students to study and conduct research anytime, even beyond library hours.

Library Events and Workshops

The library hosts weekly sessions such as book talks, research workshops, and reading programs to engage students and promote knowledge-sharing. These interactive events are open to all registered users.

What We Do

We are dedicated to fostering a love for reading, learning, and knowledge-sharing. Our library offers a vast collection of books, including fiction, non-fiction, academic resources, and the latest bestsellers. Whether you're a student, researcher, or book enthusiast, we provide a peaceful and well-equipped space for study and exploration.
